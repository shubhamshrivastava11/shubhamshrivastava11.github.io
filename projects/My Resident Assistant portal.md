MyResidentAssistant.com is a revolutionary platform designed to ease the transition for students moving to a new city or country. This comprehensive information service provides students with a single destination for finding essential services like car rentals, apartment leases, furniture, and books, reducing the stress of relocating and focusing on studies.

<hr>

<pre>
Overview
MyResidentAssistant.com focuses on improving the student experience by offering a one-stop platform for essential services. The platform consolidates information on car rentals, apartment leasing, furniture purchases, and book buying into a single user-friendly application, available on both Android and iOS. It helps students manage their transition to a new environment more efficiently, alleviating the challenges of moving and academic pressures.

Features
- Personalized Service Links: Users log in with a valid student ID and course registration details to access links to service providers.
- Integrated Chatbot: Assists users in finding specific information and services based on their needs.
- User-Friendly Interface: Simplifies navigation to essential services and reduces the need for multiple platforms.

Tech Stack
- Visualization Tools: Power BI, ERD plus, PMP tools
- Prototyping: Figma for UX design, Lucid charts

Key Milestones
- Data Collection: Develop user data ingestion pipelines for efficient data processing.
- Integration: Create APIs to connect users with service providers.
- Testing: Conduct thorough testing to ensure functionality and user satisfaction.
- Launch: Initial pilot at Oakland University with plans to expand based on demand.

Key Visualized Metrics Include:
- Subscription Plan Insights: Tracks user subscriptions and engagement with different services.
- Geographic Distribution: Visualizes user locations to tailor regional services and ads.
- Payment Method Analysis: Analyzes payment preferences to optimize service offerings.
- Service Usage Analysis: Monitors which services are most accessed to improve platform features.

Analysis
MyResidentAssistant.com aims to streamline the student experience by offering essential services in one place. This approach reduces the burden on students, allowing them to focus on their studies. The platform will initially be free for Oakland University students, with a subscription model planned for wider availability after the pilot phase.

PROJECT OBJECTIVE
MyResidentAssistant.com is designed to manage student needs efficiently, offering a capital investment opportunity of $179,269. Initially free for Oakland University students, it will transition to a subscription-based model at $15 per user for three months post-pilot. The project starts on 10/01/2022 with an expected launch date of 4/30/2023.
